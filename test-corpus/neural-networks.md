# Neural Networks

Neural networks are computing systems inspired by biological neural networks in the human brain.

## Architecture

A neural network consists of layers of interconnected nodes (neurons). Dr. Geoffrey Hinton pioneered many key techniques at the University of Toronto.

### Convolutional Neural Networks

CNNs are specialized for processing grid-like data. Yann LeCun at Meta AI Research developed early CNN architectures.

### Recurrent Neural Networks

RNNs process sequential data. They're widely used in natural language processing and time series analysis.

## Training

Networks are trained through backpropagation and gradient descent. Large models require significant computational resources, often using GPUs from NVIDIA.

## Applications

Neural networks power modern AI systems including GPT, BERT, and other large language models from companies like Google AI and OpenAI.
